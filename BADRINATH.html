BADRINATH TEMPLE

BADRINATH or BADRINARAYANA temple is a hindu temple dedicated to lord vishnu. it is situated in the town of BADRINATH in uttarakhand,india. the temple is also one of the 108 divya desams dedicated to vishnu holy shrines for Vaishnavas-who is worshipped as badrinath. the temple is one of the holy places where the hindus offer oblations to ancestors with the help of the priests. devotees visit the temple to worship in front of the image of badrinath in the sanctum and have a holy dip in ALAKNANDA RIVER.
